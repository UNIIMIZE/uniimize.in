PPC & SEO

Scaling E-commerce Revenue by 300% in 6 Months

Client E-commerce Brand
Industry Retail / Apparel
Services SEO, PPC
Timeline 6 Months
Organic Traffic +300%
Return on Ad Spend 2.5x
Customer Acquisition Cost -40%

The Challenge

When this premium apparel brand approached Uniimize, they were heavily reliant on paid social ads with a Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) that was eating into their margins. Their Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) had dropped below 1.5x, making it nearly impossible to scale profitably.

Additionally, their organic search presence was nearly non-existent. Despite having a robust catalog, technical SEO errors and a lack of optimised category pages meant they were missing out on thousands of high-intent searchers every month.

The Strategy

We implemented a two-pronged approach: stabilize and optimise paid acquisition while building a sustainable organic traffic engine to lower blended CAC over time.

First, we conducted a comprehensive technical SEO audit. We identified severe crawlability issues, duplicate content from faceted navigation, and a lack of targeted keywords on primary category pages. We mapped high-volume, transactional keywords to these pages and optimised metadata, headers, and on-page content.

Simultaneously, we restructured their Google Ads account. We moved away from broad match keyword dumping and implemented a granular, intent-based campaign structure. We also introduced Performance Max campaigns with high-quality creative assets to capture conversions across Google's entire network.

The Execution

  • Resolved 1,200+ technical SEO errors (404s, redirect chains, canonical issues).
  • Optimised 45 core category pages with high-intent keyword targeting.
  • Restructured Google Ads account into segmented Search and Performance Max campaigns.
  • Implemented dynamic retargeting to re-engage cart abandoners.
  • Launched an automated review generation email sequence to build social proof.

Our Extended Methodology

At Uniimize, achieving these exceptional results is not a matter of luck; it is the product of our rigorously tested, data-driven methodologies tailored specifically for your market. When taking on a new campaign, whether it be for an emerging startup or an established enterprise, we begin with an exhaustive auditing phase. We refuse to guess what might work. Instead, we dive deep into historical analytics data, scrutinise competitor strategies, and map out the entire customer journey to identify friction points and untapped opportunities.

Technical Foundations and Infrastructure

No marketing campaign can reach its full potential if it is built on a flawed foundation. Our technical team works closely with our strategists to ensure that the client's digital infrastructure is flawless. We obsess over Core Web Vitals, ensuring that pages load instantaneously and provide a seamless user experience across all devices. We implement advanced schema markup to help search engines understand the context of the content perfectly, securing rich snippets and improving organic click-through rates. By resolving indexation bloat, configuring optimal crawl budgets, and setting up robust server-side tracking, we guarantee that every pound spent on acquisition is tracked accurately and attributed to the correct source.

Intent-Driven Content Engineering

Traffic without intent is merely a vanity metric. Our content strategies are engineered to attract users who are actively seeking a solution. We move beyond basic keyword volume analysis, looking at the nuanced search intent behind each query. Are users looking for information, comparing products, or ready to purchase? We map our content directly to these intent stages. We produce high-authority pillar pages, detailed cluster content, and conversion-optimised landing pages. This semantic clustering not only establishes our clients as topical authorities in the eyes of Google but also guides the user naturally down the funnel, drastically reducing the sales cycle.

Algorithmic Bidding and Paid Media Synergy

In the realm of paid advertising, we leverage the full power of machine learning while maintaining strict human oversight. We structure Google Ads and Meta campaigns to feed the algorithms the highest quality data possible. By integrating offline conversion tracking and pipeline revenue data back into the ad platforms, we train the systems to bid not just for cheap leads, but for high-Lifetime-Value (LTV) customers. Furthermore, our channels do not operate in silos. The insights gleaned from high-converting search terms in PPC are immediately fed into our organic content strategy, while our top-performing organic pages are leveraged as high-quality landing environments for our paid traffic. This synergy creates a compounding growth loop that competitors find impossible to replicate.

Continuous Conversion Rate Optimisation (CRO)

Acquiring traffic is only half the battle. Our continuous Conversion Rate Optimisation (CRO) processes ensure that we are constantly extracting more value from the existing traffic. We deploy advanced heatmapping, session recording, and A/B testing protocols to systematically eliminate friction on the website. Whether it's redesigning the checkout flow, optimising the placement of trust signals, or refining the ad copy, our iterative testing approach guarantees that conversion rates climb steadily over time. This holistic, data-obsessed methodology is the bedrock upon which all our client successes are built.
